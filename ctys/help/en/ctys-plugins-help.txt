
NAME

ctys-plugins - verifies of current install status

SYNTAX
======

ctys-plugins


   [-d <common-debug-option>]
   [-E ]
   [-h]
   [-H <help-options>]
   [-l <remote-user>]
   [-L <remote-user>]
   [-R <remote-host>]
   [-t <plugin-type>]
   [-T <plugin-preload-typelist>]
   [-V]
   [-X]
   [-Z <set-sudo-ksu>]
   <list-of-target-acounts>
